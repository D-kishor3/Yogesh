Who We are?
Kannan Project Traning Center

Kannan Project Center is a training institution which started in the year 2013. The prime aim of our institution is to provide quality education and training to students who are pursuing their career in the field of Information Technology and also to working professionals who wish to upgrade their skills.

We provide training on various programming languages like Java, .NET, PHP, Python, etc. We also offer project training to students pursuing their final year projects. The projects undertaken by our students are real-time projects which give them an opportunity to work on live projects and gain practical experience.

Our institution is located in the heart of the city and is easily accessible by all means of transport. We have a team of experienced and qualified faculty members who are dedicated to imparting knowledge to students. The course curriculum is designed keeping in mind the latest industry trends and requirements.


We believe in providing quality education at an affordable price and our courses are reasonably priced. We also offer discounts and scholarships to deserving students. So if you are looking for a good training institute for your final year project or for upgrading your skills, then do visit us at Kannan Project Center!
